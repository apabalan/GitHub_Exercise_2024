About Betta Fish

Betta fish, also known as Siamese fighting fish, are popular pets known for their vibrant colors and flowing fins. They originate from Southeast Asia and are often kept in small aquariums or bowls.

Care Guide

To keep your Betta fish healthy and happy, it's essential to provide them with a suitable environment. Here are some tips:
